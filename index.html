About Me

Hi, I’m Hoda! I’m a data scientist with a passion for machine learning, applied statistics, and health data science. Currently, I’m pursuing my Master’s in Data Science, after earning a Bachelor’s in Statistics. My research focuses on AI-driven medical diagnostics, with a particular emphasis on melanoma detection.

Beyond my academic work, I’m an author passionate about making science accessible to young minds. Through engaging biographical novels, I bring the stories of influential scientists to middle school students, inspiring the next generation with the achievements of those who have shaped the world of science.

Outside of research and writing, I enjoy reading, practicing yoga, and exploring new ideas that fuel my creativity. I also love visiting historical and cultural sites, immersing myself in their stories and significance.
