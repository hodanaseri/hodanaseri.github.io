About Me

Hi, I'm Hoda Naseri! I'm a data scientist with a passion for machine learning, applied statistics, and health data science. I hold an M.Sc. in Data Science from Tarbiat Modares University and a B.Sc. in Statistics from Shahid Beheshti University.

My research focuses on AI-driven medical diagnostics, with a particular emphasis on melanoma detection using multimodal dermoscopic and pathological data. I collaborate with dermatologists at Razi Hospital (Tehran University of Medical Sciences) and have worked as a research assistant at the National Brain Mapping Lab (NBML).

Beyond research, I'm an author passionate about making science accessible to young minds. I write biographical novels about influential scientists for middle school students and have translated Simply Artificial Intelligence into Persian.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading, practicing yoga, visiting historical sites, and exploring new ideas that fuel my creativity.
